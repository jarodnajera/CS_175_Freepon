Our Mission:
Freepon is commited to serving students who are struggling with food insecurity and worry about when their next meal will be. With Freepon, hot and ready meals come straight to students with no extra charge. Started by five college students who are going through their undergraduate program, we too know what it's like to be worrying about money and food insecurity. We are proud to be working with our sponsors as we continue to make a difference in students' lives. Together, we can make food more accessible to all and help each other flourish!
